üîç Motivation & Impact

Engineering simulations are computationally expensive and require expert interpretation. As part of my Research Assistantship in the Civil & Environmental Engineering department at UIUC, I developed a lightweight 3D visualizer to streamline vehicle stress analysis and support more efficient evaluation of AI-based predictive models.

The tool allows users to choose from one of four predefined vehicle meshes and upload their own trained AI model. It then renders a side-by-side comparison of real vs AI-predicted stress values on the selected mesh, enabling users to visually assess prediction accuracy, identify spatial anomalies, and explore error patterns through interactive 3D comparison. A built-in LLM assistant (powered by Ollama 3.2) supports natural language queries about stress magnitude, discrepancies, and model behavior ‚Äî achieving 78.33% accuracy across 20 evaluation queries.
